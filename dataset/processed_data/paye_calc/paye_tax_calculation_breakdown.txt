### Detailed Math Breakdown

1. Start with your income: **‚Ç¶4,000,000**
2. First ‚Ç¶800,000 is tax-free

   * Remaining: ‚Ç¶4,000,000 - ‚Ç¶800,000 = ‚Ç¶3,200,000
3. Next ‚Ç¶2,200,000 is taxed at 15%

   * Tax = ‚Ç¶2,200,000 √ó 0.15 = ‚Ç¶330,000
   * Remaining: ‚Ç¶3,200,000 - ‚Ç¶2,200,000 = ‚Ç¶1,000,000
4. Final ‚Ç¶1,000,000 is taxed at 18%

   * Tax = ‚Ç¶1,000,000 √ó 0.18 = ‚Ç¶180,000
   * Remaining: ‚Ç¶0 (all income accounted for)
5. Add up all taxes: ‚Ç¶0 + ‚Ç¶330,000 + ‚Ç¶180,000 = ‚Ç¶510,000
